Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
  2. Parboil the rice (partly cook it).
  3. Layer marinated chicken and rice in a pot.
  4. Add saffron-infused water, fried onions, and mint leaves.
  5. Seal the pot with dough and cook on low flame until done.
  6. Garnish with more fried onions and fresh herbs.
