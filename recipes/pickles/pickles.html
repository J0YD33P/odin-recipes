Indian Lemon Pickles

Description

Nimbu ka achar, also known as Indian Lemon Pickle, is a delightful condiment with spicy and tangy flavors. It‚Äôs often served in Indian restaurants alongside poppadoms and poppadom dips.

Ingredients

  • 4 large lemons
  • Salt (as needed)
  • Red chili powder (adjust to taste)

Instructions

  1. Rinse and cut 4 lemons into small pieces.
  2. Mix the lemon pieces with salt and red chili powder.
  3. Transfer to a glass jar and store in a cool place.
